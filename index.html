About Us

Welcome to our unofficial Nelson Mandela School cafeteria ordering website, developed by Vibbodh Somani. Our aim is to simplify your meal ordering experience and enhance your daily routine at the cafeteria. We hope to make your visits more convenient and enjoyable.

Features

A modern UI

An upgrade from our previous website, offering a sleeker design and improved User Experience.

Automation

Our goal with this new system is to reduce wait times in line, automate and enhance the food ordering process. We also want to make the staff’s work less tedious.

Data Protection

We minimize data storage and build upon established website practices. All code for this website is publicly accessible on GitHub.

Learning

School students will actively contribute to the ongoing development of this website, fostering valuable learning opportunities and hands-on experience in web development.
